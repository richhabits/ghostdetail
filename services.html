Coating · 5-year warranty
Nano Banana Ceramic
From £149 add-on · £399 standalone
5-year manufacturer warranty

9H hardness ceramic coating, hand-applied, fully cured in our climate-controlled studio bay. The coating that all our long-term clients run.

What you get
  • Paint depth readings — confirm clear coat is healthy enough to coat
  • Full decontamination prep — iron remover, tar remover, clay bar (mandatory before any coating)
  • Single-stage machine polish minimum — coating locks in whatever's underneath, so we always polish first
  • IPA panel wipe — strips polish residue so coating bonds properly

  • 5-year warranty — registered in your name, transferable on resale
