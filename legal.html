About Ghost Detail Autos

Ghost Detail Autos is a private vehicle care studio based at Sandown Road, Watford WD18, Hertfordshire, operated by Romeo Cruz and Ferrel Cruz.

We provide detailing, paint correction and ceramic coatings directly at the studio. We coordinate mechanical work, MOT bookings, alloy refurbishment, window tinting, number plate supply, vehicle sourcing, executive transport and event hire through vetted third-party partners.

Scope of Services

Ghost Detail Autos delivers two categories of work:

Direct Services — detailing, paint correction, ceramic coating, PPF, interior detailing, Ghost Pass memberships and Ghost Vision assessments. These are performed by Ghost Detail Autos directly at the Watford WD18 studio.

Coordinated Services — MOT, mechanical work, PDR, alloy refurbishment, window tinting, number plates (3D/4D and show plates), respray work, car sourcing and customisation, executive transport, fleet hire and wedding/event hire. These are coordinated by Ghost Detail Autos through trusted third-party trade partners. Ghost Detail Autos acts as the customer's agent in arranging these services and inspecting the work where appropriate.

Pricing & Quotes

Prices for direct services are set by Ghost Detail Autos and shown on the website. These may change from time to time and the current published rate at the point of booking applies.

Prices for coordinated services are subject to current market rates from our trade partners and may vary based on vehicle, condition, materials, parts availability and location. We provide quotes after consultation with you and the relevant trade partner. All such quotes are estimates until work is approved.

Any "From £X" pricing is indicative and represents the typical entry-level cost. Final pricing depends on the specific job scope and may be higher or lower.

We will not authorise work above an agreed quote without explicit written confirmation from you (WhatsApp, email or signed quote).

We will, however, advocate on your behalf in the event of any dispute and inspect work before vehicles are returned to you where practical.

Where we name a partner type or licence (e.g. "DVLA-registered" or "licensed PHV operator"), this refers to the partner organisation, not Ghost Detail Autos itself.

Number Plate Supply Notice

Road-legal number plates are supplied through our DVLA-registered partner (RNPS scheme). To order road-legal plates we require:

  • Your V5C registration document (or sufficient evidence of vehicle ownership)
  • Proof of identity (driving licence, passport or utility bill)

Show plates and replica plates are available without these requirements but are strictly for off-road, display, exhibition or theatrical use only. It is illegal to fit show plates to a vehicle used on public roads in the United Kingdom. By ordering show plates from us you confirm you will not fit them to a road-going vehicle.

Window Tinting Legal Notice

UK law sets minimum visible light transmission (VLT) values for the front of motor vehicles:

  • The front windscreen must let through at least 75% of light
  • The front side windows must let through at least 70% of light
  • Rear side windows and rear windscreen are not regulated

We will not coordinate or fit film that takes a vehicle out of UK legal compliance for the regulated windows. Owners are responsible for declaring any tinting to their insurer where required.

Executive Transport & Driver Service

All chauffeur, executive transport and driver services are coordinated through our licensed Private Hire Vehicle (PHV) operator partner. The licensed partner provides the driver, vehicle and PHV insurance.

Ghost Detail Autos arranges the booking and acts as your point of contact, but does not itself drive vehicles for hire or reward. Cancellation, refund and journey terms are governed by the licensed partner's own terms, which we will share with you at quote stage.

Insurance & Liability

Ghost Detail Autos carries public liability insurance and motor trade insurance appropriate to the work it performs directly at the studio.

For coordinated services, the insurance covering the work is held by the trade partner performing the work (e.g. a body shop carries its own insurance for respray work). Ghost Detail Autos remains liable only for its own coordination, advice and any direct work it performs.

Vehicles left at our Sandown Road studio are kept securely. We recommend you ensure your own motor insurance covers the vehicle while in our care, particularly for high-value vehicles.

Bookings, Deposits & Cancellations

Direct service bookings (detailing, ceramic, paint correction, etc.) require a deposit at the time of booking, which is taken via Stripe. The deposit secures your slot and is deducted from the final invoice.

Cancellations more than 48 hours before the booking are fully refunded. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the deposit unless the slot can be re-filled. Same-day cancellations forfeit the full deposit.

For coordinated services, deposit and cancellation terms are determined by the relevant trade partner and shared with you at quote stage. Ghost Detail Autos passes any deposit through to the partner where appropriate.

Ghost Pass Membership Terms

Ghost Pass is a recurring monthly subscription billed via Stripe. Your card is charged on the same date each month from the date of your first payment.

Ghost Pass members receive priority booking, member rates on coordinated services and free collection & delivery within the Watford catchment area, subject to availability. Specific benefits at each tier are listed on the Ghost Pass page.

You may cancel at any time and your membership will continue until the end of the current billing period. Cancellations are processed via your Ghost Garage account or by emailing hello@ghostdetail.autos.

Privacy & Data Protection

Ghost Detail Autos is the data controller for personal information collected through this website. We process your data in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect: name, mobile number, email address, vehicle registration, vehicle make/model, MOT and service history, postcode, conversation history with our AI personas, and any details you provide about service requirements.

Why we collect it: to provide you with a quote, to coordinate services, to send service-related communications, to verify your vehicle (via DVLA's public lookup API), and to send marketing communications where you have opted in.

Who we share it with: only with the specific trade partner required to deliver the service you have requested. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for unrelated marketing purposes.

How long we keep it: active customer records are retained for the duration of our relationship plus 6 years for tax and accounting purposes. Marketing-only records are deleted on request or after 2 years of inactivity.

Your rights: you can request access to, correction of or deletion of your personal data by emailing privacy@ghostdetail.autos. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Ghost Detail Autos is registered with the Information Commissioner's Office for data protection (registration in progress at the time of writing).

Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies and similar storage technologies. We use the minimum necessary, and we ask for your consent before setting non-essential ones.

Essential cookies: session tokens for the booking flow, login state for the customer area, CSRF protection, and your cookie consent preference itself. These are always on because the site cannot function without them. Lawful basis: legitimate interest under UK GDPR.

Analytics cookies (off by default): if you accept these, we use anonymous, aggregated stats to understand which pages people read and where they drop off. No individual tracking, no profiling. Lawful basis: consent.

Marketing cookies (off by default): if you accept these, we allow Stripe (for payment integrity), our AI receptionist ARIA (for conversation context), and any ad pixels we may use to remember context across pages. Lawful basis: consent.

How to control cookies: use the cookie banner that appears on your first visit, or click the cookie preferences link in our footer. You can change your mind at any time. You can also clear cookies in your browser settings.
