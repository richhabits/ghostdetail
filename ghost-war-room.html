JADE Technical

JADE's technical knowledge base. Ask her anything about paint, alloys, leather, dents, ceramics. She gives the real answer, not the marketing answer. Also shows her recommendation log from the site.

Technical Lookup
Paint Knowledge
Swirl marks — micro-scratches in clear coat
Orange peel — factory texture, not a defect
Holograms — wrong polishing technique
Paint depth — factory clear: 90-140 microns
Safe correction threshold: ~60 microns
Matte paint — no polish, specific chemistry only
Alloy Knowledge
Diamond-cut — machined face, CNC lathe repair only
Powder coat — filler + respray repair viable
Corrosion pitting — turn before it reaches metal
Trade rate: £45-65/wheel
Client price: £85-110/wheel
Leather Knowledge
Nappa — full-grain, absorbs stains, needs conditioning
Dakota — corrected-grain, more durable
Merino wool — dry extraction only, no steam
Alcantara — suede brush only, no steam direct
Bolster wear — dye loss, needs recolour not cleaning
Dent / Bodywork
PDR works — paint intact, accessible from behind
PDR fails — cracked paint, body line, filler history
PDR avoids — respray history on records
Fill + respray — £400-800+/panel via bodyshop
Rule: always try PDR before involving a bodyshop
